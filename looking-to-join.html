Thinking about joining?

HYPC welcomes all types of dinghy sailor from complete beginners to experienced racers. There is at least one safety boat on the water for all club arranged sailing.

All sailing is in mono hulled dinghies. Unfortunately we don't have the facilities for catamarans and trimarans.
