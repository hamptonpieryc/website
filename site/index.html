Hampton Pier Yacht Club

Situated in the lively coastal resort of Herne Bay, Hampton Pier Yacht Club is RYA Affiliated. We offer every facility for dinghy sailors and those just want to enjoy the beach and water other ways, maybe as a kayakers or open water swimmers.

The Club was founded in 1964, and has since developed into a modern and well equipped sports facility. All members have 24*7 access to the changing rooms and boat stores. We sail most weekends from late March until early October with evening sailing on Wednesdays during the summer

Safety is a number one priority. The Club has at least one well equipped safety patrol boat on the water at all times during organised events with VHF radio links to shore.

Sailors

Our sailing calendar offers a mix of racing, training, crusing and general sailing. Our main activity is dinghy racing but the Club also serves as a useful base for those who just prefer sailing their boats in a safe, controlled environment. For new and inexperienced sailors there is a free training programme based on the RYA Level 2 course. For those without their own dinghy, there are Club boats to hire for a small fee.

Non Sailors

We actively encourage those who don't sail but want to enjoy the water in other ways, including kayers, canoeist, paddle boarders and open water swimmers. The beach by the Club is generally quieter than the main beach and the water is partially sheltered by the old Hampton Pier.

We plan to invest in dedicated kayak/canoe/paddleboard storage, by at the moment space is limited. If you are interested in storing these, please contact us.
