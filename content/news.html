Hampton Pier Yacht Club awarded £500 from the Opportunities Fund
March 2023

The club was recently awarded two grants from the "Opportunities Fund" for activities that improve the safety and wellbeing of the local community. £200 was donated towards the cost of a replacement battery for the defibrillator and £300 towards the new safety boat trailer. We would like to pass on our thanks to Grace Paget and Councillor Peter Vickery-Jones for supporting the club.
