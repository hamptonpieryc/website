HPYC now allow dogs in the Club House
April 2024

We recently changes our rules to allow Members to bring their well behaved dogs into the Club House. This has been debated within the Club for a while as a rule change that is welcomed by families with pet dogs. Previously, they would need at least one person outside caring for their dog. Not so much of a problem on a nice day, but as we all know the Great British Summer does not guarantee fine weather.

If you are planning to bring your dog, please respect the feelings of other members who are not always dog lovers and ensure your pet is on a short lead at all times. For more information please Contact Us.

HPYC Hosting Pier to Pier Swim
July 2023

For the second year running, Hampton Pier Yacht Club will be hosting the annual ActiveLife Pier to Pier Swim. Registrations and secure bag storage will be at the Clubhouse. Competitors will also have use of the Club's changing rooms and toilets, plus refreshments and "energy bar" snacks will be available. Membership of the Club is open to non sailors including kayakers and open water swimmers, so this a good time to take a look at the facilities if you are interested. Please note that fees are discounted by 50% if joining in July.

Mini Discover Sailing Day
June 2023

The forecast is looking great for the weekend - blue skies and light winds. Perfect for beginners or those who haven't sailed for a long time, so HPYC is running a mini "Discover Sailing" day on Saturday 24th June . No experience needed - just bring sunscreen, a change of clothes and some shoes you don't mind getting wet (old trainers are perfect), we have the rest. Feel free just to turn up on the day, but if you could let us know beforehand that would be great as it will help us plan. For those joining on the open days we offer a 50% discount on the annual membership. Email to secretary@hpyc.org.uk if you are thinking of attending or have questions. Please note that as this is being run as part of our regular training sessions, we will only be on the water from 3PM until between 4 and 5PM.

Social Calendar
Coronation Party
April 2023

Please come along to have some fun and to support the Club as we celebrate the new King's coronation on Bank Holiday Monday, 8 May , starting at 14:00 . This is open to all, not just Club members, so please also feel free to circulate the attached poster as widely as you may wish to include family and/or friends - the more the merrier! There is no charge for attendance.

It's entirely optional but everyone is invited to wear red, white and blue and there will be a competition for the best crown/hat with a royal theme, with separate prizes for adults and children.

Fun activities we are planning include "Pin the crown on the King", "Royal Who am I?", etc, and, as long as the weather lets us remain outside, Boules (Petanque) and maybe even a Tug of War!

Food will, as recorded on the poster, be kept simple with freshly prepared Hot Dogs, Hamburgers, Pizzas and Ice Cream. Vegetarian options will, of course, also be available. And please don't forget we have a well-stocked bar with prices that are amongst the best in town.

There is no need to make firm commitments in advance. But, to help gauge numbers for catering, if you are interested in joining in it would be greatly appreciated if you would send a short message to the social team at socialhpyc@gmail.com.

HPYC awarded £500 from the Opportunities Fund
March 2023

The Club was recently awarded two grants from the "Opportunities Fund" for activities that improve the safety and wellbeing of the local community. £200 was donated towards the cost of a replacement battery for the defibrillator and £300 towards the new safety boat trailer. We would like to pass on our thanks to Grace Paget and Councillor Peter Vickery-Jones for their support of the Club.
