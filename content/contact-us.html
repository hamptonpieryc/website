Contact us

Please email to secretary@hpyc.org.uk if you need to contact us.

We always welcome enquiries from those interested in joining the club. If you want to come and have a look around or chat to someone please feel free to drop by any time that we are sailing. There is no need to let us know in advance, but if you do we can make sure a club member is there to greet you.

If you need to write, our postal address is the footer.
