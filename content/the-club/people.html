Throughout his career he has never missed an opportunity to go sailing, including sand-yachting in the desert in Saudi Arabia! A member of HPYC since 2015, Chris has served in a variety of roles on the GMC. He loves the Club and is wholeheartedly committed to its continuing growth and development.
