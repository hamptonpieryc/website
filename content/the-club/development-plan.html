Development Plan

We have a 5-year development plan that is available on request.

The primary focus of the Club is to be the sailing club of choice for local families. To ensure the long term future of the Club it is important to ensure that the Club remains strong with a thriving membership. To do this we feel it is necessary to firstly ensure that the Club has a prominent local presence. The best advert for the Club however is to ensure we have strong attendances for Club racing. To achieve this, the Club will provide the necessary training to allow our members to reach their full sailing potential. We will provide RYA syllabus based training covering both the on water and off water aspects necessary for safe and effective sailing.
