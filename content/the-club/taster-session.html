Taster Sessions

We run short taster sessions with experienced helms. You will be out sailing the water for around 20 to 30 minutes. These are ideal for those who have never sailed , or haven't been out on the water for a long time.

What do I need to do
  • Pack a change of clothes, sunscreen and some old shoes / trainers.
  • Depending on the weather, adding a "spray jacket" (a lightweight windproof/waterproof top) will help keep you warm.
  • We have life-jackets and helmets. If you have your own wetsuit, we recommend bringing it along - we have some spare wetsuits, but the selection is quite limited
  • Wetsuits are not normally needed, but they can help keep you warm if it is a cold day If you have your own wetsuit, we recommend bringing it along - we have some spare wetsuits, but the selection is quite limited
  • If you can, Contact us beforehand and let us know what you hope to get out the session. It helps us plan for the day.
