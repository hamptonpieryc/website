Taster Sessions

We run short taster sessions. These are ideal for those who have never sailed , or haven't been out on the water for a long time.

There is no need to book in advance

What do I need to do
  • Pack a change of clothes, some sunscreen and some old shoes / trainers.
  • Depending on the weather, a "spray jacket" (a lightweight windproof/waterproof top) will help keep you warm.
  • We have life-jackets, helmets and collection of spare wetsuits. If you have your own wetsuit, we recommend bring it along.
No experience needed • Pack a change of clothes and some sunscreen • Book on the day • See details

We have spare life jackets, so all you need is suitable clothing to wear in the boat - "suitable" of course does depend on the weather, however as a minimum we recommend old shoes/trainers, a change of clothing in case you get wet and a splash proof top. Unfortunately we don't have spare wetsuits, but of course you can bring your own.
