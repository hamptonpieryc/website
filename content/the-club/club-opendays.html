Open Day 16th May

Our Open Day for the 2026 season will be on the 16th of May, from 11AM to 4PM.

Membership fees are discounted 25% for those joining at the open day.

Try a Taster Session
No experience needed • Pack a change of clothes and some sunscreen • Book on the day • See details

The Open Days are an opportunity to see the Club, talk to members and, if the weather is kind, get out sailing with an experienced sailor. We have spare life jackets, so all you need is suitable clothing to wear in the boat - "suitable" of course does depend on the weather, however as a minimum we recommend old shoes/trainers, a change of clothing in case you get wet and a splash proof top. Unfortunately we don't have spare wetsuits, but of course you can bring your own.

There is no need to book, but feel free to Contact Us before hand if you have any specific questions. We will be posting more updates closer to the day on the website, Instagram and Facebook.
