Open Day 16th May

Our Open Day for the 2026 season will be on the 16th of May, from 10AM to 4PM.

Membership fees are discounted 25% for those joining at the open day.

Try a Taster Session
No experience needed • Pack a change of clothes and some sunscreen • Book on the day • See details

The Open Days are an opportunity to see the Club, talk to members and, if the weather is kind, get out sailing with an experienced sailor for a Taster Session.

There is no need to book. But it helps us plan if you can let us know in advance if possible, or if you have any question, using the Contact Us page. We will be posting more updates closer to the day on the website, Instagram and Facebook.
