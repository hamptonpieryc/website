Our 2025 Sailing Programme is now available online. The first sail of the year is on 30th March

2025 Sailing Calendar​

We sail both Saturday and Sunday on most weekends from late March until early November. There is also Wednesday evening sailing during the summer months.

Please note that if you have a myclub account you can sign in directly from the top right of the calendar, and then select "Duties" from the main menu to view the up-coming duties.

You can also download a calendar file. Open this file to add the dates automatically to the calendar on your personal mobile/tablet/computer.
