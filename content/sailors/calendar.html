Sailing Calendar

Please view our 2026 Calendar. The first sail of the year will be 29th March, and we sail until mid November (for the brave)!. We sail both Saturday and Sunday on most weekends. There is also Wednesday evening sailing during the summer months.

Please note that if you have a myclub account you can sign in directly from the top to all see duties and race results.

You can also download an ical (.ics) file. Open this file to add the dates automatically to the calendar on your personal mobile/tablet/computer.
