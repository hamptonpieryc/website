Boat Storage

There is boat storage on the grass bank to the side of the Clubhouse and also on the beach behind the safety boat garage. There is also a locked sail store. Spaces are limited and only available to sailing members. See the Fees page for prices.

If you are looking for a place to store kayaks, canoes or paddle boards unfortunately we don't have the space for these at the present. However we are looking at options so please Contact Us to let us know you are interested.
