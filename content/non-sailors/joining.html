Thinking about joining?

We are not just a sailing club. We welcome those who want to use the club a base for other activities and those who want to just socialize without getting their feet wet.

Enjoy the sea views from our bar and make use of the Clubhouse which is conveniently placed within walking distance of Herne Bay pier. All club members have 24*7 access to the toilets, changing rooms and showers.

Kayaks and Canoes

The water by the Club is partly sheltered by the old Hampton Pier and a nice place to start a trip up or down the bay. Kayakers and canoeist can simply join as social members or of course with a sailing membership and mix both activities. There are no restrictions on when you can go out on the water, but it is your responsibility to make sure you are safe.

We are looking at options for the secure storage of kayaks and canoes but currently space is very limited. If you need storage please Contact us. Free parking is normally available outside peak periods or there is paid parking in the nearby Hampton Pier car park.

 The Club only permits the use of non-powered water craft and reserves the right to restrict some types. Contact us if unsure. We ask that you keep clear of the dinghies during sailing events. The safety boats will only be out on the water when sailing events are on and only actively monitor the dingies.

Swimming and SUP

For open water swimmers and paddle boarders the Club offers a convenient place to change and a safe, sheltered beach which is normally quieter than the main beach. We are about 15 minutes walk from Herne Bay Pier and free parking is normally available outside peak periods, or there is paid parking in the nearby Hampton Pier car park. There are no restrictions on when you can go out on the water, but it is your responsibility to make sure you are safe.

 We ask that you keep clear of the dinghies during sailing events. The safety boats will only be out on the water when sailing events are on and only actively monitor the dingies.
