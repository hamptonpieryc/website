Hampton Pier Yacht Club

Situated in the lively coastal resort of Herne Bay, Hampton Pier Yacht Club offers every facility for dinghy sailors. We are more than a sailing club, and actively encourage anyone who want to enjoy the water. And for those who want to keep their feet dry, there is an active social calendar throughout the year and our members only bar with wonderful views over the sea. All members have 24*7 access to the changing rooms and sail stores.

The Club was founded in 1964, and has since developed into a modern and well equipped sports facility. We sail most weekends from late March until early October with evening sailing on Wednesdays during the summer

Safety is a number one priority. The Club has at least one well equipped safety patrol boat on the water at all times during Club events with VHF radio links to shore.
Sailors

Our sailing calendar offers a mix of racing, training, crusing and general sailing. Our main activity is dinghy racing but the Club also serves as a useful base for those who just prefer sailing their boats in a safe, controlled environment. For new and inexperienced sailors there is a free training programme based on the RYA Level 2 course. For those without their own dinghy, the Club has a number of dinghies to hire for a small fee.

Non Sailors

We actively encourage those who don't sail but want to enjoy the water in other ways, including kayers, canoeist, paddle boarders and open water swimmers. The beach by the Club is generally quieter than the main beach and the water is partially sheltered by the old hampton pier. All members have 24 * 7 access to the changing rooms and toilets.
