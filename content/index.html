Hampton Pier Yacht Club

Situated in the lively coastal resort of Herne Bay, Hampton Pier Yacht Club offers every facility for the keen dinghy sailor. We have a full programme of dinghy racing, cruising and fun sailing with members of all ages and abilities. The Club was founded over 40 years ago and has since developed into a modern and well equipped sports facility with a keen and active membership.

Our main activity is dinghy racing but the Club also serves as a useful base for those who just prefer sailing their boats in a safe, controlled environment. It is not necessary to be a skilled sailor to join our Club or even know how to sail. The Club has a number of boats which members can use whilst training.

Safety of our members remains a number one priority. The Club has well equipped safety patrol boats on the water at all times during Club events with VHF radio links to shore.

The Club will welcome all members who wish to use non-powered craft, including paddle boards, kayaks and canoes.
