Weather

The Club has an Ecowitt weather station on the roof. There is a public webpage with Live data, or you may install the Ecowitt App (available for Apple and Android), create a personal account and then you can use the app features to find HPYC on the map and personalise your settings.

Ecowitt has limited forcasting. We rely upon a number of weather forcasting sites including Windguru , BBC Weather, the Met Office and the Isle Of Sheppey Sailing Club's Weather Cam (they often have similar conditions to us).
