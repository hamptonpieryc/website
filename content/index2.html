Hampton Pier Yacht Club

Situated in the lively coastal resort of Herne Bay, Hampton Pier Yacht Club offers every facility for dinghy sailors. We are more than a sailing club, and actively encourage anyone who want to enjoy the water, including kayakers, canoeist, paddle boarders and open water swimmers. And for those who want to keep their feet dry, there is an active social calendar throughout the year and our members only bar with wonderful views over the sea. All members have 24*7 access to the changing rooms and sail stores.

The Club was founded in 1964 years ago and has since developed into a modern and well equipped sports facility. We sail most weekends from late March until early October with evening sailing on Wednesdays during the summer.
