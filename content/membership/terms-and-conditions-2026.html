Abandoned Boats

Where a Boat parking or storage fee is a month or more in arrears any boat or trailer parked on club premises will be treated as an abandoned or unauthorised and its removal will be requested in writing. Where the owner fails to remove the boat, the Club may sell the boat/trailer to recover any monies due to the Club.
