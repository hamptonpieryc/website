Joining and Renewal

To apply to join the club, please fill in the form below and either hand in at person in the club house or send a scanned copy to the membership secretary at: membership@hpyc.org.uk.

Existing members wishing should complete the renewal form. Please note that late renewals (after 1st March) may have a surcharge applied.

Sailing Memberships

Please complete either the joining or renewal form. For new members requiring boat storage, please check that space is available and ensure the necessary insurance is in place (details are on the form). For existing members with boat storage, please check that the insurance is still valid.

For your safety, we recommend that the medical forms are also completed. These are kept in sealed envelops and only opened in the event of an accident or emergency.
