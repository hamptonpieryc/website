Joining and Renewal

2026 membership renewals are due from 1st January 2026 and should be paid before 1st March. As mentioned in earlier newsletters, this year we will be sending out online renewal in early January. Full details will be circulated soon. Alternatively, use the existing paper "Membership Form". The 2026 prices are on the Fees page. Please note that currently the peper membership form has the 2025 prices, please adjust these manually

>The 2026 Sailing Calendar will be released soon.

To apply to join the club, please fill a "Membership" form and either hand in at person in the Clubhouse or send a scanned copy to the membership secretary at membership@hpyc.org.uk. Payment can be made via bank transfer, cheque or debit/credit card at the Clubhouse - details are on the forms.

Sailing Memberships

Please complete the membership form below. For new members requiring boat storage, please check that space is available and ensure the necessary insurance is in place - details are on the form. For existing members with boat storage, please check that your insurance is still valid.

For your safety, we recommend that the relevant medical forms are also completed. These are kept securely in the Clubhouse in sealed envelopes and only opened in the event of an accident or emergency.

 The Club is run by the members for the members. Sailors are expected to help with the Sailing Duties.
