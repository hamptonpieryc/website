Online Renewals

This year we are starting an online renewal. You should have already received communication about this via email and WhatsApp.

This is optional. You may still use the paper forms as before - see the Joining and Renewal page.
How this works

You should receive an email with the title "Hampton Pier Yacht Club Membership Renewal". This is from no-reply@myclub.run. This is the same email address used for duties and newsletters. As always, please double-check your spam/junk folder if you cannot find the email.

To continue, click on the link in the email. The steps are:

  • Confirm your post code
  • Review the details on the form
  • Pick a renewal option:
    1. Renew now
    2. Renew with changes
    3. Sorry, not renewing
  • Complete the form:
  • If renewing, send you payment by bank transfer, cheque or cash. Current there is no online payment option.

A confirm email will be sent out once the renewal has been processed and any payment required received. These are processed manually, so it may be a few days before the email goes out.

If you have myclub login (this is all duties users and some socials, you can also access see this form your Setting Page.
