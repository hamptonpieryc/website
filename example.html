HPYC welcomes dinghy sailors, anyone who wants to enjoy the water as a swimmer, kayaker or paddle boarder and those looking for a friendly social club.

We support dinghy sailor from complete beginners to experienced racers. There is at least one safety boat on the water for all club arranged sailing. Sailing is in mono hulled dinghies only.

New to sailing

Never been in a dinghy? HPYC run a free training programme over the season to take complete beginners to confident sailors. We start the training on our club boats, and if you don't have your own dinghy these can rented for a small fee as you progress. Our training is run by RYA approved trainers assisted by experienced sailors and is based around the RYA Level 1 course.
